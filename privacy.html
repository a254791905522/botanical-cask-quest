Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Botanical Cask Quest is an offline single-player craft distillation simulation game. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle your information.

No Data Collected

Botanical Cask Quest does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app contains:

  • No analytics or tracking SDKs
  • No advertising networks
  • No in-app purchases
  • No user accounts or sign-in
  • No network requests of any kind
  • No camera, microphone, location, or contacts access

Local Data

All game progress (level unlocks, stars earned, sound and music toggle preferences) is stored locally on your device using iOS NSUserDefaults. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Children's Privacy

The app is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. No personal information is collected from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated date.
