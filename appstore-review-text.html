App Store Localizable Text
Operate a 1890s copper still, cut heads hearts and tails, age in oak casks, and bottle 50 craft spirits across 5 historic regions.
botanical,cask,distill,spirits,whiskey,brandy,vodka,rum,workshop,craft,offline,simulation
Botanical Cask Quest is an offline craft distillation simulation. Set in the 1890-1920 era, you operate copper stills across five historic spirit regions, crafting gin, whiskey, brandy, vodka, and rum.

HOW TO PLAY
Each level is a distillation contract. The workshop shows a copper boiler, column, flame, thermometer, three collection jars (heads, hearts, tails), a barrel, and a bottle.
- Select the correct raw material for the spirit type (grain for whiskey, grape for brandy, molasses for rum, juniper for gin).
- Select process steps in order: Ferment, Distill, Botanicals, Age, Bottle.
- Use Fire Low and Fire High to heat the still to the correct boiler temperature.
- Watch the thermometer and proof. Cut Heads, collect Hearts, then cut Tails at the right moment.
- Age in the correct cask, dilute to target proof, and bottle.

Correct material and process flow completes the level with "Spirit Success". Too many mistakes results in "Spirit Failed".

50 CONTRACTS
Journey across five historic distillery regions, each with 10 levels:
- London (1-10): Gin - London Dry, Old Tom, Plymouth, Sloe, Monkey 47.
- Ireland (11-20): Whiskey - Single Malt, Pot Still, Triple Distill, Sherry Cask, 10 Year.
- Cognac (21-30): Brandy - VS, VSOP, XO, Borderies, Grande Champagne, Vintage.
- Poland (31-40): Vodka - Triple Distilled, Gold Filtered, Premium Rye, Wheat, Flavored.
- Barbados (41-50): Rum - White, Aged 3 Years, Dark, Spiced, Cask Strength, 15 Year Reserve.

TUTORIAL
Levels 1-3 are guided training: heat to 78C, cut Heads, collect 25 mL of Hearts.

DISTILLATION SYSTEM
- Boiler temperature: 0-120C. Condenser: 5-25C.
- Heads: first volatile compounds, cut early.
- Hearts: ethanol with desired flavors, the main spirit.
- Tails: heavier compounds, cut late.
- Proof tracked in real time.

STILL TYPES
Pot Still (flavorful spirits), Column Still (high purity), Continuous Still (efficient), Vacuum Still (delicate botanicals).

CASK AGING
Bourbon (vanilla, caramel), Sherry (dried fruit, nuts), New Oak (tannin, spice), Wine (grape, berry), Rum (tropical sweetness).

WEATHER EFFECTS
Each level has ambient weather (Clear, Fog, Heavy Rain, Blizzard, Thunder) that affects still equilibrium temperature.

FEATURES
- 50 levels across 5 historic regions.
- 4 still types, 5 cask types, 3 distillation fractions.
- 5 weather conditions affecting still behavior.
- Guided tutorial for the first 3 levels.
- Level select grid with sequential unlock.
- Fully offline. No ads, no IAP, no accounts, no tracking.
- Portrait, iPhone only.

Botanical Cask Quest is a calm, atmospheric puzzle about reading the still, cutting at the right moment, and crafting the perfect spirit.
